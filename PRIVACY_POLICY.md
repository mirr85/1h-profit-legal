Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application 1H PROFIT (the "App") processes information.

The App is designed for adult professional drivers and provides real-time profitability analysis based on information displayed on the user's device screen.

The App operates without user accounts and without a Developer-operated backend server.

Core Architecture and Data Minimization

The App is designed according to the principle of data minimization:

No account registration is required.

The Developer does not operate a backend database for user data.

Core processing is performed locally on the user's device.

The App does not use any third-party SDKs for crash reporting or analytics (such as Firebase Crashlytics or Google Analytics).

No technical logs, device identifiers, or usage statistics are transmitted to the Developer or third parties.

The Developer does not build user behavior profiles and does not sell personal data.

Categories of Data Processed

A) Screen Content (Media Projection API)

The App accesses a limited region of the screen (ROI – region of interest) to detect trip offer details (such as price, time, distance, and address text) displayed within taxi-service applications.

Processing details:

OCR and calculations are performed locally on the device.

Screen frames are processed in volatile memory only.

Screen images are not saved to persistent storage.

The Developer does not receive screen images.

B) Geocoding and Map Data

Recognized address text may be sent to MapTiler to obtain geographic coordinates.

Obtained coordinates may be sent to GeoNames to retrieve a city name.

Data transmitted consists solely of address/place text and/or coordinates necessary for geocoding.

The Developer does not attach user names, emails, device IDs, advertising identifiers, or personal identifiers to these requests.

The User acknowledges that by using geocoding features, limited data (address text) is processed by these providers under their own terms.

C) Local App Storage

The App may store locally on the device:

Technical dictionary entries used for OCR correction and filtering.

Map tile cache for performance optimization.

This data remains exclusively on the user's device and is not transmitted to the Developer.

D) Subscription Status (Google Play Billing)

If the user subscribes via Google Play:

Payment processing is handled entirely by Google.

The Developer does not receive or store credit card or banking details.

The App may receive subscription status information (e.g., active or expired) from Google Play services for feature activation.

No external subscription validation server is used.

Permissions Used

Screen Capture (Media Projection) Purpose: Reading trip offer details for calculation. Legal basis: User consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

Display Over Other Apps Purpose: Displaying calculated metrics and map preview overlay.

Internet Access Internet access is strictly limited to performing geocoding requests via MapTiler and GeoNames and checking subscription status via Google Play Billing. No background data synchronization or telemetry occurs.

No advertising SDKs are included. No third-party analytics SDKs are included.

Legal Bases (GDPR / RODO)

Processing is based on:

Article 6(1)(b) – performance of a contract (providing requested functionality).

Article 6(1)(a) – user consent (screen capture and permissions).

Article 6(1)(f) – legitimate interest (ensuring functionality and stability without profiling).

Data Retention

Screen data is processed transiently in memory and not stored persistently.

Local dictionary entries and cache remain stored until the user clears App data or uninstalls the App.

Subscription status is retained only as required for subscription functionality.

International Transfers

Geocoding providers may process requests outside the European Economic Area under their own GDPR-compliant safeguards.

All communication with third-party services is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
